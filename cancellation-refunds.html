Cancellation & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Overview

This Cancellation & Refunds Policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancelling services and processing refunds through NeighShopGlobal's ServiceMate platform. All refunds are processed through our secure payment gateway system.

2. Payment Gateway Integration

Important: NeighShopGlobal requires payment gateway integration for:

  • Service Payments: Accepting payments from clients for services booked through the platform
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Generating monthly payment links for easy subscription management and recurring payments

All transactions are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway to ensure safe and reliable payment processing.

3. Service Cancellation by Customer

3.1 Pre-Paid Bookings

If a customer cancels a booking for which payment has already been processed:

  • Before Service Provider Acceptance: Full refund (100% of the amount paid) will be processed through the payment gateway
  • After Service Provider Acceptance: Partial refund of 75% of the amount paid (25% cancellation fee applies)
  • After Service Has Started: No refund, unless service provider fails to complete the service as agreed

3.2 Post-Payment or Pay-Later Bookings

For bookings where payment is scheduled after service completion or at the time of service:

  • No cancellation charges apply if cancelled before service provider acceptance
  • If cancelled after acceptance, platform-specific policies may apply

4. Cancellation by Service Provider

If a service provider cancels an accepted booking:

  • Customer receives a full refund (100% of any amount paid) processed automatically through the payment gateway
  • Service provider may face penalties as determined by the platform owner
  • Platform will attempt to find a replacement service provider if available

5. Refund Processing

5.1 Payment Gateway Processing

All refunds are processed through our integrated payment gateway system:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • UPI/Wallets: 2-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days

5.2 Refund Timeline

Once a refund is approved:

  • Refund request is processed within 2-3 business days through the payment gateway
  • Actual credit to your account depends on your bank/payment provider
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed

6. Monthly Subscription Payments

For platform owners using monthly subscription services:

  • Monthly payment links are generated automatically through the payment gateway
  • Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime, with refunds processed according to our refund policy
  • Refundable within 7 days of initial purchase
  • Setup fees are non-refundable

7. Service Quality Issues

7.1 Incomplete Service

If a service provider fails to complete the service as agreed:

  • Customer is entitled to a full or partial refund based on the extent of service completion
  • Refund amount will be determined by the platform owner after reviewing the case
  • Refunds are processed through the payment gateway

7.2 Poor Service Quality

If a customer is dissatisfied with the quality of service:

  • Customer should report the issue within 24 hours of service completion
  • Platform owner will review the complaint and may offer a partial refund or service redo
  • Refund decisions are at the discretion of the platform owner

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Log into your account on the platform
  2. Navigate to your booking history
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel" or "Request Refund"
  5. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation
  6. Refund will be processed automatically through the payment gateway
  7. Contact customer support if you need assistance

9. Payment Gateway Security

All payment transactions and refunds are processed through secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. Your payment information is encrypted and securely handled according to industry standards.

10. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Contact the platform owner's customer support
  • Provide relevant documentation (receipts, photos, communication records)
  • Platform owner will review and make a final decision
  • NeighShopGlobal may assist in dispute resolution if requested by the platform owner

11. Contact for Refund Inquiries

For refund-related questions or issues, please contact:

  • Platform Support: Contact the customer support of the specific marketplace platform
  • NeighShopGlobal Support: info@neighshopglobal.com | WhatsApp: +91 8307802643

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation & Refunds Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
