Shipping Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Important Notice

This Shipping Policy page is provided for payment gateway compliance requirements. ServiceMate by NeighShopGlobal is a digital service marketplace platform that does not engage in physical shipping of goods or products.

1. Overview

ServiceMate by NeighShopGlobal is a service-based marketplace platform that connects customers with service providers for on-site and remote services. We do not ship, deliver, or handle any physical goods or products.

2. No Physical Shipping

NeighShopGlobal does not engage in physical shipping of any kind. Our platform exclusively facilitates:

  • Digital service bookings and transactions
  • On-site service appointments (services performed at customer location)
  • Remote/digital services (consultations, digital work, etc.)
  • Service provider management and payment processing

All transactions on our platform are for services only, not physical products.

3. Payment Gateway Integration

Payment Processing: NeighShopGlobal requires payment gateway integration for:

  • Service Payments: Accepting payments from clients for services booked through the platform. All service payments are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Generating monthly payment links for platform owners to manage recurring payments easily. This facilitates seamless subscription management.

All payments are processed digitally through secure payment gateways. No physical goods are involved in any transaction.

4. Service Delivery

Since we provide services (not products), "delivery" refers to service completion:

  • On-Site Services: Service providers visit the customer's location to perform the service
  • Remote Services: Services are delivered digitally or through remote consultation
  • Service Completion: Services are considered "delivered" when completed as agreed between customer and service provider

5. Service Provider Responsibilities

If a service provider needs to bring tools, equipment, or materials to perform a service:

  • These items are brought by the service provider directly to the service location
  • NeighShopGlobal is not involved in transporting or shipping these items
  • Any arrangements for materials or equipment are between the customer and service provider

6. Why This Policy Exists

This Shipping Policy page is maintained to comply with payment gateway requirements and industry standards. While we do not ship physical goods, this policy ensures transparency and compliance with payment processing regulations.
