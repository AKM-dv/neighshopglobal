Shipping Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

ServiceMate by NeighShopGlobal is a service marketplace platform that connects customers with service providers. This Shipping Policy applies to any physical goods or materials that may be delivered as part of service provision.

2. Service-Based Platform

ServiceMate primarily facilitates on-site and remote services. Most services are performed at the customer's location or remotely, and do not involve physical shipping of products.

3. Physical Goods Delivery

In cases where service providers need to deliver physical goods, parts, or materials as part of their service:

  • Delivery terms and timelines are agreed upon between the customer and service provider
  • Shipping costs, if applicable, are included in the service quote or charged separately
  • Delivery timelines vary based on service type and location

4. Payment Gateway Integration

Important: NeighShopGlobal requires payment gateway integration for:

  • Service Payments: Accepting payments from clients for services booked through the platform. All service payments, including those for services that may involve physical goods, are processed securely through our integrated payment gateway.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Generating monthly payment links for platform owners to manage recurring payments easily. This facilitates seamless subscription management.

5. Delivery Timeline

For services involving physical goods:

  • Standard delivery: 3-7 business days (varies by location and service type)
  • Express delivery: 1-3 business days (subject to availability and additional charges)
  • Same-day delivery: Available for select services in specific areas (subject to availability)

6. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs, when applicable:

  • Are clearly displayed during the booking process
  • Are included in the total service cost
  • Are processed through the payment gateway along with service payments
  • May vary based on location, weight, and delivery speed

7. Tracking

For services involving physical goods delivery:

  • Tracking information is provided when available
  • Customers receive notifications about delivery status
  • Service providers update delivery status through the platform

8. Delivery Issues

8.1 Delayed Delivery

If delivery is delayed:

  • Customers will be notified of any delays
  • Service providers are responsible for communicating delays
  • Refunds may be available for significant delays (see Cancellation & Refunds Policy)

8.2 Damaged or Lost Items

If physical goods are damaged or lost during delivery:

  • Customers should report the issue immediately
  • Service providers are responsible for replacement or refund
  • Refunds are processed through the payment gateway

9. International Shipping

International shipping availability depends on:

  • Service provider capabilities
  • Local regulations and restrictions
  • Additional shipping costs and customs duties

10. Return Shipping

For services involving physical goods that need to be returned:

  • Return shipping policies are determined by individual service providers
  • Return shipping costs may be the responsibility of the customer or service provider
  • Refunds for returned items are processed through the payment gateway

11. Contact for Shipping Inquiries

For shipping-related questions or issues:

  • Service Provider: Contact the service provider directly through the platform
  • Platform Support: Contact the customer support of the specific marketplace platform
  • NeighShopGlobal Support: support@neighshopglobal.com

12. Changes to Shipping Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Shipping Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
